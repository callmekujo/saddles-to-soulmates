It’s been a little more quiet between us, and yeah, I’ve felt that. But even with the distance, I’ve always kept cheering you on from afar.

From student to doctor to business owner, you’ve built something incredible with your own two hands. And we’ve both watched you grow into a strong, passionate, fiercely capable woman. It’s been incredible to see you rise, even from the sidelines.

I’m excited for who you are, what you’ve built, and what’s still ahead.

Marriage is the next wild ride, and lucky for you, you’ve got the right partner and the right heart to make it one hell of an adventure.

Jordan
